About

Emily Pattni is the Curator for the Future of Print initiative at Arizona State University.

She earned her B.A. in English Literature with a concentration in Grant and Technical Writing and her M.Ed from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. She will graduate with her Masters in Library Science from the University of Washington in the Summer of 2019.
